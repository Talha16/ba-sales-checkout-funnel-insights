## Business Context
NovaCart is an online retailer aiming to improve conversion rates across its e-commerce checkout funnel. While traffic to the site is healthy, a significant number of users drop off before completing a purchase.

## Problem Statement
The business lacks clear visibility into where customers are dropping out of the checkout funnel and why. Without data-driven insights, it is difficult to prioritize improvements to the checkout experience.

## Business Objective
The objective of this analysis is to:
- Understand user behavior across the checkout funnel
- Identify key drop-off points
- Define metrics that indicate checkout performance
- Provide insights that support future optimization decisions

## Checkout Funnel Stages
The checkout funnel is defined as:
1. Product Viewed
2. Add to Cart
3. Proceed to Checkout
4. Enter Shipping Information
5. Enter Payment Information
6. Order Confirmation

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Funnel conversion rate (stage-to-stage)
- Cart abandonment rate
- Checkout abandonment rate
- Payment failure rate
- Average order value (AOV)

## Business Questions
- At which stage do users most frequently abandon the checkout process?
- Is there a significant drop-off between shipping and payment steps?
- How does payment failure impact overall conversion?
- Are certain stages disproportionately affecting revenue?
